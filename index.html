How it works

Up and running in minutes

Whether you're posting a job or building a business profile, TradeHire is designed to get you connected fast.

1

Create your account

Sign up as a homeowner, tradesperson, or supplier. Your account type determines what you can do — post jobs, build a business profile, or list your store.

2

Build your profile or post a job

Homeowners describe what they need. Tradespeople set up a detailed business profile complete with services, location, portfolio photos, and trade licences. AI helps you stand out.

3

Connect and get to work

Browse verified profiles, send direct messages, respond to job postings, and leave reviews when the work is done. Everything in one place.
